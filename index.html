SET @ Kent State University

Security Enhancement Technology (SET)

Security has become one of the core values of the cyber world.

We look at various aspects of security, including Integrity, Authenticity, Availability, Privacy, Confidentiality, and Verifiability.

We are looking for motivated students to join us. Feel free to contact setlab.cs@gmail.com if you are interested.
